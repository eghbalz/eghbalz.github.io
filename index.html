Research Interests

  • Adversarial Machine Learning

    Generative Adversarial Networks, Adversarial robustness.

  • Representation Learning

    Learning representations from signals and sequences.

  • Learning from data with incomplete information

    Unsupervised and semi-supervised learning, learning under distribution mismatch, learning from weak labels.
