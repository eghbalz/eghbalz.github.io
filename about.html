About Me

Hamid's Personal Homepage

I am a Postdoc Researcher at the Linz Institute of Technology (LIT) AI LAB.
I received my PhD degree in 2019 on Representation Learning and Inference from Signals and Sequences. In 2014, I joined the Institute of Computational Perecption at the Johannes Kepler University of Linz. Before that, I worked as a researcher in the area of Statistical Speech Synthesis and Speaker Recognition at Ozyegin University in Istanbul, Turkey. In 2012, I received my Master’s degree in Computer Engineering - Artificial Intelligence from the Shiraz State University, Iran.
